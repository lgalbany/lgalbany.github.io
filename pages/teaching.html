Teaching

I have 2 years experience in lecturing as a teacher assistant at the Physics Department of Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB) during 2008/09 and 2009/10 seasons. Since September 2019, I am also teachinng at Universidad de Granada. I have also given tutorials in schools for PhD students: the Guillermo Haro Advanced School on Integral Field Spectroscopy Techniques and Analysis in Mexico 2014, and the 2nd SELGIFS Advenced School on Integral-Field Spectrosocopic Data Analysis in Madrid 2016. I obtained the Certificat d’Aptitud Pedagògica (CAP) in the Institut de Ciències de l’Educció of UAB during 2006/07 season, which is a requirement in Spain to teach at high school level. I finished the certifiction of the program Formació Docent en Educació Superior (FDES) at the Unitat d’Innovació Docent en Educació Superior (IDES) of UAB during 2008/09-2010/11 seasons, and obtained the certification of Associate Level after completing the program Preparation for the Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) Classroom: An Introduction to Evidence-Based STEM Teaching at the Center for the Integration of Research, Teaching and Learning (CIRTL) of the University of Pittsburgh.
